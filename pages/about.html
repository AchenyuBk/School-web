About PHIBMAT

PHIBMAT's History

Phibmat University, though a relatively young institution with nearly four years of history, has rapidly established itself as a beacon of excellence in higher education. Founded with a vision to provide transformative education, Phibmat University opened its doors to its first cohort of students in 2020. Since then, it has grown from a fledgling university into a dynamic and innovative academic community.



Phibmat University is now a dynamic and forward-thinking institution committed to providing a transformative educational experience. Our mission is to empower students to become leaders, innovators, and responsible global citizens. Located in a vibrant community, Phibmat University offers a diverse range of programs and opportunities that cater to the interests and aspirations of every student.

Our Mission

At Phibmat University, our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to become leaders, innovators, and responsible global citizens. We are dedicated to fostering a culture of excellence in teaching, research, and service, while nurturing the intellectual, personal, and professional growth of our students. Through our comprehensive academic programs and vibrant campus life, we aim to prepare our students for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our Vission

PHIBMAT 'The University of excellencce' aspires to become a leading university in the quality and relevance of its teaching, research and services. The University is dedicated to the continuous quest for excellence in research, the promotion of moral and human values, and service to the community.
